# Section 4 — Shift Structure & Operational Model

## Overview

ShiftFlow is designed around Home Depot DFC shift structures.

All planning, history, reporting, templates, fairness calculations, and assignment tracking must be associated with a specific shift key.

The shift key is a core business concept and must never be treated as a simple text field.

Every plan belongs to:

* Department
* Shift Key
* Date

Example:

Outbound + Key 1 + May 29, 2026

Inbound + Key 2 + May 29, 2026

These are separate plans.

---

Schedule:

Monday–Thursday

Day shift operations.

---

Schedule:

Monday–Thursday

7:30 PM – 5:30 AM

Night shift operations.

---

## Key 3

Schedule:

Friday–Sunday

8:00 AM – 8:30 PM

Purpose:

Weekend operations.

---

# Key Selection

Before creating a plan, the leader must select:

1. Department
2. Key

Example:

Department:
Outbound

Key:
Key 1

The application then loads:

* Key 1 associates
* Key 1 templates
* Key 1 history
* Key 1 rotation data

---

# Associate Key Assignment

Every associate must have a default key assignment.

Example:

John
Department: Outbound
Default Key: Key 1

Maria
Department: Inbound
Default Key: Key 2

Alex
Department: Outbound
Default Key: Key 3

---

# Overtime Logic

Associates may work outside their normal key.

Example:

Maria
Default Key: Key 2

Works overtime on:

Key 1

The system must support:

* Overtime assignment
* Temporary inclusion in another key plan
* History tracking
* Fairness tracking

The associate's default key must remain unchanged.

---

# Planning Model

The application supports two independent planning models:

## Outbound Planning Model

Task-based planning.

Examples:

* CL
* FLR
* OBL
* PA/TL
* Tile Overbox
* Oblong Overbox
* Zone 1 Loader
* Zone 2 Loader
* Zone 3 Loader
* All Zone Pacer Loader
* Housekeeping

The system generates assignments based on:

* Available associates
* Certifications
* Templates
* Fairness rules
* Overtime
* Special assignments

---

## Inbound Planning Model

Operational task planning.

Primary inbound tasks:

* Unload
* Put Away
* Middle Mile
* Support Outbound
* Housekeeping

Inbound planning must also support dock-door operations.

The system should load a configurable dock door list and allow leaders to activate only the doors needed for the current shift.

---

# Active Dock Doors

The dock door list is configurable.

Example:

* Door 635
* Door 622
* Door 618
* Door 640

The system must not hardcode dock doors.

Leaders select which doors are active for the day.

Example:

Active today:

* 635
* 622
* 618

The auto-plan engine only considers active doors.

---

# Special Operational Assignments

The planning engine must support:

## Overtime

Associate temporarily working another key.

---

## Middle Mile

Can be owned by:

* Inbound
* Outbound

When one department owns Middle Mile, the other department should display:

Handled by Inbound

or

Handled by Outbound

---

## ICQA Support

Outbound may provide support to ICQA.

These associates are considered assigned and should not be automatically assigned elsewhere.

---

## Training

The system supports:

Trainer + New Hire pairs.

Example:

Trainer:
John

New Hire:
Mike

Task:
FLR

Equipment:
Pacer

Training pairs are treated as active assignments.

---

## Housekeeping

Housekeeping can occur at any time.

Examples:

* Aisle 304
* Aisle 408
* Pallet Cleanup
* Trash Run
* Dock Sweep

Housekeeping tasks must be configurable.

---

# Assignment Lifecycle

An associate may move through multiple assignments during a shift.

Example:

8:00 AM
Unload

10:30 AM
Put Away

1:00 PM
Support Outbound

4:00 PM
Housekeeping

All assignment changes must be recorded in history.

---

# Assignment Statuses

Supported statuses:

* Assigned
* Active
* Available
* Break
* Lunch
* Training
* Overtime
* Completed

These statuses should be visible on dashboards and TV displays.

---

# Available Pool

Every plan must maintain an Available Pool.

The Available Pool contains associates who:

* Are present
* Are not currently assigned
* Are not on break
* Are not on lunch
* Are not inactive

Leaders can quickly assign available associates to new tasks.

---

# Operational Philosophy

ShiftFlow should prioritize:

* Fast planning
* Operational flexibility
* Fairness
* Visibility
* Reassignment speed
* Real-time updates

The platform must support rapid changes without requiring leaders to rebuild plans from scratch.
