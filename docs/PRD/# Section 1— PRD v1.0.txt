# ShiftFlow DFC 5523

## Product Requirements Document (PRD) v1.0

### Executive Summary

ShiftFlow DFC 5523 is a real-time warehouse labor planning and coordination platform designed to replace Excel spreadsheets, screenshots, whiteboards, and manual labor assignment processes currently used in distribution center operations.

The primary purpose of ShiftFlow is to simplify the daily planning process for warehouse leaders while improving visibility, fairness, operational efficiency, communication, and workforce management.

The platform supports separate workflows for Inbound and Outbound operations while maintaining a consistent planning experience across all shift keys.

ShiftFlow is specifically designed around real warehouse operational workflows including:

* Daily labor planning
* Dock door assignments
* Trailer unloading
* Put Away assignments
* Loading operations
* Picking operations
* Housekeeping assignments
* Middle Mile operations
* ICQA support
* Overtime support
* Training assignments
* Live labor movement
* Shift meetings
* TV display presentations

The system will generate labor plans automatically using configurable business rules while still allowing leaders full manual control over assignments.

The application will maintain assignment history, support fair labor rotation, track associate activities over time, and provide leadership with visibility into workforce allocation decisions.

ShiftFlow is intended to be production-ready, enterprise-grade software suitable for daily operational use inside a distribution center environment.

### Primary Objectives

1. Reduce time required for leaders to create daily plans.

2. Eliminate dependency on Excel screenshots and whiteboards.

3. Provide live visibility of labor assignments.

4. Improve fairness through assignment rotation tracking.

5. Allow rapid labor reallocation throughout the day.

6. Provide TV-friendly presentation mode for daily meetings.

7. Track associate assignment history.

8. Support inbound and outbound planning workflows.

9. Support configurable tasks, equipment, templates, and dock doors.

10. Create a scalable platform capable of supporting future facilities beyond DFC 5523.

### Success Criteria

The platform will be considered successful if:

* Leaders can generate a daily labor plan in less than 5 minutes.
* Assignment changes appear on TV displays in real time.
* Fair rotation warnings reduce repetitive assignments.
* Labor history can be viewed for any associate.
* New tasks and equipment can be added without code changes.
* The system remains usable on desktop, tablet, mobile, and TV screens.
* The platform can replace existing spreadsheet and whiteboard workflows.
