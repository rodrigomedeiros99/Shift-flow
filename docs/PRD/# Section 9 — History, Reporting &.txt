# Section 9 — History, Reporting & Analytics

## Purpose

ShiftFlow shall maintain complete historical records of planning decisions and operational activities.

The History system serves three primary purposes:

1. Fair Rotation Recommendations
2. Operational Visibility
3. Reporting & Analytics

The system shall preserve historical records while allowing leaders to quickly search and analyze workforce assignments.

---

# History Types

ShiftFlow shall maintain two separate history systems:

## Planned Assignment History

Represents the original labor plan created and published by leadership.

Examples:

Monday

John → CL

Maria → FLR

Carlos → Zone 1 Loader

This history represents leadership intent.

---

## Activity History

Represents actual labor movement throughout the shift.

Example:

John

08:00 AM → CL

11:30 AM → Zone 1 Loader

02:00 PM → Housekeeping

04:00 PM → Support ICQA

This history represents operational reality.

---

# Associate History View

Leaders must be able to select any associate.

Example:

Select Associate:

John

The system displays:

* Planned Assignment History
* Activity History
* Timeline View
* Assignment Frequency
* Equipment Usage
* Department History

---

# Planned Assignment View

Example:

John

Monday → CL

Tuesday → Loader

Wednesday → FLR

Thursday → Tile Overbox

This view is primarily used by:

* Fair Rotation Recommendations
* Auto-Plan Generation
* Workforce balancing

---

# Activity Timeline View

Example:

John

08:00 AM → CL

11:30 AM → Loader

02:00 PM → Housekeeping

04:00 PM → ICQA Support

This view is primarily used for:

* Audits
* Labor tracking
* Operational analysis

---

# Department Filters

History must be separated by department.

Options:

* Inbound
* Outbound

Example:

Outbound History

Only outbound associates and assignments appear.

Inbound History

Only inbound associates and assignments appear.

---

# Key Filters

History must be separated by shift key.

Options:

* Key 1
* Key 2
* Key 3

Example:

John

Outbound

Key 1

Monday → CL

Tuesday → Loader

Wednesday → FLR

# Assignment Frequency Analysis

The system shall calculate:

How often an associate performed a specific task.

Example:

John

Last 30 Days

CL → 10

FLR → 5

Loader → 7

Tile Overbox → 3

This data supports rotation recommendations.

---

# Team Distribution Analysis

Leaders should be able to view task distribution across the team.

Example:

CL

John → 10

Maria → 8

Carlos → 9

This helps identify assignment imbalances.

---

# Rotation Analytics

The system should display:

Rotation Score

Examples:

Excellent

Good

Needs Improvement

The score is informational only.

Leadership remains responsible for final assignment decisions.

# Export Capabilities

Future versions should support:

* PDF Export
* Excel Export

---

# Analytics Philosophy

Analytics exist to provide visibility.

The purpose is to:

* Improve planning
* Improve fairness
* Improve decision-making

Analytics should never replace leadership judgment.

Leadership remains responsible for operational decisions.
